About Starsy

Welcome to Starsy, the elegant star system mapper designed with sci-fi RPG game masters in mind. The interactive star system designer brings the cosmos to your fingertips, allowing you to craft detailed stellar configurations for your gaming sessions or creative projects.

Starsy stands out as a specialized astronomy visualization tool that simplifies the complex process of stellar cartography. With an intuitive interface, you can quickly create linear orbit maps of any star system you envision, from scientifically plausible to imaginatively fantastic.

Developed by game-masters for game-masters, to enhance sci-fi RPG experiences, add astronomical depth to storytelling without getting lost in technical complexity.

Whether you're plotting the next adventure for your tabletop campaign or designing an entire galactic sector, this stellar cartography tool provides the perfect balance of simplicity and detail. As a linear orbit map creator, Starsy focuses on clear visual representation rather than overwhelming complex physics simulations.
